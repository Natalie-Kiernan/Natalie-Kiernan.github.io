About Natalie

Natalie will make your project a success with her can't-fail attitude and unending committment to visual aesthetics for creative messaging. She has completed identity branding, full-page newspaper ads, museum schedules, posters, programs, magazine spreads, information flyers, animated-for-web click-ads and animated-for-web birthday cards.

Natalie is proficient in Adobe Creative Cloud's Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and Lightroom,as well as the Microsoft Office Suite. Her software and soft skills have been developed through professional administrative office work after she obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Management from St. John's University, NY. In additon to her studio projects, she was a reporter for The Student Union Newspaper, The SUN, to fulfill her personal sense of civic duty.

Now with an Associate's Degree in Graphic Design and Illustration, she has her sights set on a meaningful internship with an organization that puts her skills to use on their current graphic design needs.

Natalie graduated May 2026 summa cum laude from CT State Community College - Tunxis where she got to know her talented and devoted Graphic Design and Illustration Professors. She plans on learning and perfecting Web Design and Motion Graphics skills this summer and continuing with a Bachelor's Degree in Visual Communication Design.
