About Natalie

Natalie will make your project a success with her can't-fail attitude and endless committment for creative messaging through visual aesthetics. Here you can view her large body of work for print and the web. With her identity branding, full-page newspaper ads, museum schedules, organizational anniversary posters, program booklets, magazine spreads, information flyers, animated-for-web click-ads and animated-for-web birthday cards, she graduated summa cum laude from CT State Community College - Tunxis where she was taught by talented and devoted industry-working Graphic Design and Illustration Professors.

Natalie is proficient in and has created all these designs with Adobe Creative Cloud's Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and Lightroom; She was formerly a NYC-based computer program instructor teaching Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Access, and HTML classes after using these professionally for years. Her soft and software skills have been honed through professional administrative office work in and out of NYC, before and after she obtained a Bachelor of Science in Management from St. John's University, NY.

To fulfill her personal civic duty, in additon to her studio projects, she volunteered for her college's newspaper, The Student Union Newspaper (The SUN) as a student reporter. After graduating from St. John's, she was a Covenant House counselor in their 30-day shelter for young mothers.

Holding an Associate of Science in Graphic Design and Illustration, she has her sights set on a meaningful internship with an organization she is eager to learn from and that puts her skills to use on their current graphic design needs. She is now continuing skill-development in Web Design and Motion Graphics and continuing with a Bachelor of Science in Visual Communication Design.
