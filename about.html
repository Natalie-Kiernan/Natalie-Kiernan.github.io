About Natalie

I’m Natalie, a graphic artist committed to clear, purposeful design and continuous growth in my craft. I’m actively seeking a meaningful internship where I can contribute, learn, and push my skills further through real‑world creative challenges. My approach is simple: show up fully, work hard, and deliver design that communicates with intention. I bring a can’t‑fail attitude, a strong sense of visual problem‑solving, and a genuine dedication to producing work that supports the goals of the project and the people behind it.
