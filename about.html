About Natalie

WHY HIRE ME?

I will make your visual project a success with my can't-fail attitude and tireless committment to aesthetically pleasing visual messaging. Dont' take my word for it; Please hit "Gallery" above; See my digital body of work for print and the web. With this body of work, I earned my Associate of Science in Graphic Design and Illustration, summa cum laude, instructed by gifted and respected industry-working Graphic Design and Illustration Professors.

Adobe Creative Cloud applications: Ai, Ps, Id, Lr were used exclusively for all my portfolio designs. I have used the Microsoft Office Suite academically and professionally for decades and professionally taught others. I have excellent written and verbal skills; I interviewed and wrote articles for my college newspaper as a student reporter. I started learning newspaper spread layout of images and articles; I used Indesign and could put the articles into such layout. My soft and software skills stem from many years of a professional administration career. My natural business sense is solidified with a B.S. in Management. My love of people and sense of civic responsibility, fulfilled by my volunteer work. I was a Covenant House counselor working in their NYC shelter for young mothers in crisis, fresh out of business school.

I am actively seeking a meaningful internship that puts my design skills to the test directly on client graphic design projects. I am currently increasing skill-development in Web Design and Motion Graphics. I shall be obtaining a Bachelor of Science in Visual Communication Design in the near future.
