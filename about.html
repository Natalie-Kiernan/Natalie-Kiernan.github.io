About Natalie

Natalie's committment to creative messaging through visual aesthetics, plus a can't-fail attitude, will make your visual project a success. Her digital body of work for print and the web can be viewd through the gallery, above. Find her book covers, posters, identity branding, full-page newspaper ads, program booklets, magazine spread, animated-for-web click-ads, and animated-for-web birthday card. With this body of work, she earned her Associate of Science in Graphic Design and Illustration, summa cum laude, from CT State Community College and from gifted and respected industry-working Graphic Design and Illustration Professors.

Natalie has created all these designs with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and Lightroom; Formerly a NYC-based computer program instructor, she's taught Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Access, and HTML classes after using these, professionally, for years. Her soft and software skills stem from a professional administration career after she obtained a Bachelor of Science in Management from St. John's University, NY.

Natalie's sense of citizen responsibility through public service is central to her work for her college newspaper, The Student Union (The SUN). She was also a Covenant House counselor working in their shelter for young mothers in crisis after graduating from St. John's College of Business.

Natalie is actively seeking a meaningful internship, from which she can further her learning, that puts her design skills to the test directly on client graphic design projects. She is currently increasing skill-development in Web Design and Motion Graphics. She will be obtaining a Bachelor of Science in Visual Communication Design.
