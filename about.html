About Natalie

Natalie will make your visual project a success with a dedicated committment to creative messaging through visual aesthetics and a can't-fail attitude. Her digital body of work for print and the web can be viewd through the gallery tab above. Displayed are her book covers, posters, identity branding, full-page newspaper ads, program booklets, magazine spread, animated-for-web click-ads, and animated-for-web birthday card. With this work, she earned her Associate of Science in Graphic Design and Illustration, summa cum laude, from CT State Community College and from respected, gifted, and devoted industry-working Graphic Design and Illustration Professors.

Natalie is proficient in and has created all these designs with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and Lightroom; Formerly a NYC-based computer program instructor, she's taught Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Access, and HTML classes after using these, professionally for years. Her soft and software skills have been honed through professional administration work after she obtained a Bachelor of Science in Management from St. John's University, NY.

Natalie likes public service and had learned interviewing and reporting skills for her college newspaper, The Student Union (The SUN). She was a Covenant House counselor working in their shelter for young mothers in crisis after graduating from St. John's College of Business.

Natalie is seeking a meaningful internship she can learn from and that puts her design skills to the test on graphic design projects. She is currently spending her summer continuing skill-development in Web Design and Motion Graphics. She plans on rounidng out her design education with a Bachelor of Science in Visual Communication Design.
